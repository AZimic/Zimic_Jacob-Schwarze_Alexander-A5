Overwatch Releases New Hero

Blizzard releases new hero Moira; a healer with what they are calling "evasive and damage dealing-abilities". They have also made the game free to play for the remainder of the weekend. Even though this is now over, those of you with the game should queue up and see if you can master Moira!

Lots of Updates Headed Our Way

Blizzard has stated that they have planned to bring a bunch of updates to Overwatch shortly after entering 2018. They also have plans to bring back last year's event that came out on December 13th. Content includes loot boxes containing Blizzard-themed skins.

Miraculous Youngers Step Away from Overwatch

The team issued a statement saying they will be in a hiatus after competing in the Nexus Cup. Apparently, the team has been losing money all year. All players from the team plan to switch to other games.
